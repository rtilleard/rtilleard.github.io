I live in London and work at Multiverse. I grew up in rural Australia and previously studied political science, economics and economic history at the universities of Melbourne and Oxford.

My career and research has focused on how to increase access to good jobs and promote economic growth. This includes stints at the Boston Consulting Group, the Behavioural Insights Team and now in tech (previously Humu & now Multiverse).
