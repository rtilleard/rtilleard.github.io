I'm working on a few idea related to jobs, decision making and behavioural science

Decision Tools

Good decision making and implementation is hard. 70% of major projects fail to finish on time or on budget. We can do better.

From noticing a problem or opportunity, deliberating about a solution, and implementing your decision problems occur.

We can't avoid bad decisions. But we can use tools that make it more likely we will make good decisions.

At My Red Teams we're working on creating practical tools that teams can use to help them make good decisions on the projects or ideas they are working on.
